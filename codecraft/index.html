About CodeCraft

Learn by building, not by watching.

CodeCraft is a monthly hands-on workshop series at UIUC where you skip the theory and go straight to making real things — websites, games, apps — from scratch.

Every session is designed so that complete beginners leave with a fully working project they built themselves — no fluff, no filler.
