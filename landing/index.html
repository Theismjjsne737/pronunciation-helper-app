The Method

From recording
to refinement

1

Say a word or phrase

Hold to record in the coach, or drill from Mimiq's 58-word practice library across six phoneme categories.

2

Mimiq analyzes your audio

N-best speech recognition selects the most accurate transcript. Phonemes are mapped to IPA and compared to native targets.

3

Get targeted technique coaching

The AI identifies your deviation — vowel reduction, retroflex R, tone — and teaches the physical technique needed to correct it.

4

Earn XP, master sounds

Consistent sessions build your accent profile. Phonemes graduate from practicing to mastered. Your fingerprint evolves every session.
