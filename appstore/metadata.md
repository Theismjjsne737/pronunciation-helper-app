## Description (4000 chars max)

Struggling to say "Worcestershire"? Can't nail "Nguyen"? Mimiq is your personal AI pronunciation coach — available 24/7, brutally honest, and infinitely patient.

**How it works**

Just say the word. Mimiq listens, scores your pronunciation down to the phoneme level, shows you exactly which sounds you're nailing and which need work, then coaches you on how to fix them. It's like having a linguistics professor in your pocket.

**What makes Mimiq different**

Unlike flashcard apps or passive listening tools, Mimiq gives you real-time phoneme-level feedback. You'll see a breakdown of every sound in the word, hear the native reference at normal and slow speed, and watch your accuracy improve attempt by attempt on a progress chart.

**Features**

• AI Coach — have a full conversation with your pronunciation coach. Ask about any word, name, or phrase and get expert guidance tailored to your accent background.

• Phoneme Breakdown — see exactly which sounds you got right and which need work, with articulation tips for each.

• Daily Challenge — one tricky word per day keeps your skills sharp. Master words like "colonel", "quinoa", and "chipotle" that trip up even fluent speakers.

• Accent Fingerprint — Mimiq tracks your phoneme patterns over time, identifying your personal accent tendencies and targeting your weakest areas.

• Progress Charts — every word you practice builds a history. Watch your accuracy climb from 60% to 95% on the same word.

• Share Your Score — scored 98% on "Worcestershire"? Share your result card and challenge your friends.

• XP & Levels — earn experience points for every attempt. Level up from Newcomer to Master as your pronunciation improves.

• Practice Library — 58 carefully curated words across 6 difficulty tiers, from everyday mispronunciations to advanced phoneme challenges.

• Native Reference Audio — hear every word at normal speed and slow speed before you record. Know exactly what you're aiming for.

**Works for**

— Non-native English speakers refining their accent
— Native speakers who've always mispronounced certain words
— Actors, presenters, and public speakers
— Anyone preparing for job interviews, presentations, or travel

**Privacy**

Voice recordings are processed for waveform display and sent to our speech recognition service only for scoring. We do not store your voice data.
